Introduction

Rent Rahisi is a property management platform that helps landlords and property managers manage rental properties, collect rent, generate invoices and reports, and maintain tenant records.

User Registration

  1. Open the Rent Rahisi website.
  2. Click Sign Up.
  3. Enter your details.
  4. Create a password.
  5. Submit the registration form.

Login

  1. Navigate to the login page.
  2. Enter your email and password.
  3. Click Login.

Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides an overview of properties, tenants, payments, invoices, and reports.

Managing Properties

  1. Select Properties from the menu.
  2. Click Add Property.
  3. Enter property details.
  4. Save the information.

Managing Tenants

  1. Navigate to Tenant Management.
  2. Click Add Tenant.
  3. Enter tenant details.
  4. Assign the tenant to a unit.

Rent Collection and Payments

Rent Rahisi supports rent collection through M-Pesa, bank payments, and cash recording.

  • Create invoices.
  • Send invoices via SMS or Email.
  • Track paid and unpaid invoices.
  • Generate receipts.

Reports

  • Payment Reports
  • Tenant Reports
  • Property Reports
  • Financial Summaries

Property Selling Module

Users can manage plots and house sales, create installment invoices, track payments, and generate project reports.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a property?

Go to Properties and click Add Property.

How do I generate reports?

Open Reports and select the report type.

How do tenants pay rent?

Through M-Pesa, bank transfers, or cash payments.
